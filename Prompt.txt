Following Prompt was used with AI Models: Grok 4, ChatGPT GPT-5.3, Microsoft 365 Copilot GPT‑5

You are a digital forensic analyst.

I have uploaded:
1) A CSV file exported from a mobile application database
2) A TXT file containing a list of business names identified manually (ground truth)

Task:
- Check which business names from the TXT file appear anywhere in the CSV data.
- Match case-insensitively.
- Do NOT guess or infer new business names.

Output format:
1) Business names FOUND in the CSV
2) Business names NOT FOUND in the CSV

Rules:
- Only search for names listed in the TXT file.
- Do not add new names.
- Do not explain yet, just list results clearly.
